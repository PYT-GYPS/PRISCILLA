About Us

Empowering organizations and communities with knowledge and tools for gender equality and women's empowerment.

Priscilla Thindwa

Priscilla Thindwa is an experienced International Development Programme Manager with a strong background in human rights and a proven commitment to gender equality and women's empowerment.

Our agency specializes in designing and implementing gender-responsive programs, conducting in-depth research on gender dynamics, and building strategic partnerships to promote sustainable development and social justice.

We offer expertise in curriculum development, policy analysis, and community engagement, with a focus on integrating gender mainstreaming, GESI principles, and social justice across various sectors, including agriculture.

Our capabilities include providing technical expertise, developing gender mainstreaming tools, and fostering inclusive economic participation for women and marginalized groups.
